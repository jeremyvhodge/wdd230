Target Audience

The audience are people that are looking to learn what activities there are and what amenaties are available.

Personas

From Out of Town Persona: This person is not familiar with the area and is looking for information about where to stay, where to eat, and what activities there are. The Local Resident Persona: This person lives in the area and is wanting to know events are happeing in thier city.

Scenarios

A person is looking for a place to eat. A mom is looking for things to do for the family. A dad is looking for informaion on sporting events. A visitor is looking for information on mountain biking trails. A local is looking on where to float the Portneuf river.
